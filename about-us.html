How we got started

After being assigned to work as a team for the first assignment in their coding bootcamp course at North Western, Bill, Mary Ann, Julia, and Miguel found a common issue when it came to travel.

With different professional backgrounds, team Same-Page realized they wished they had more time to make the most of their experience during business trips or be able to quickly acess events across a city tailored to their interests.

This is how EXPLORO by Same-Page came to be.
